One continuous talent journey

The Talent Data Matrix shows how data collected from applicants and current employees transforms into one continuous information flow. The skill gaps identified by senior employees during their daily projects dynamically update the exact competencies the AI prioritizes when screening incoming candidates. Conversely, the fresh strengths these new graduates bring—such as AI-native diligence—flow seamlessly back into the internal workforce through the CDP Exchange as reverse-mentoring opportunities for the seniors, creating a unified cycle of organizational growth.

The Tapered Buddy Timeline
A buddy's time commitment tapers over the 3-month onboarding.
Week 1 · High touchDays 1–5 · ~30–60 min/day
Survival week: set up their environment, do the intros, explain the unwritten team norms, and have lunch together so they don't feel isolated.
Weeks 2–4 · Medium touchDays 6–30 · ~2–3 hrs/week
First small tasks. Unblock them when stuck, review early work, and kill the "dumb question" anxiety.
Months 2–3 · Low touchDays 31–90 · <1 hr/week
Long-term integration: a bi-weekly coffee on culture fit, cross-team politics, and how they feel about their growth.
